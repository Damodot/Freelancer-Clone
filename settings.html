What is trust score?

The Freelancer Trust Score is at the core of how we handle verification, trust, and payments. The Trust Score is a value that indicates to what extent we have been able to verify who a user says they are. Employers and freelancers who are the safest to work with are those who put in more effort to verify themselves to become highly trusted users. Learn more.
